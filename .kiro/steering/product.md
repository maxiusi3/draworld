# Product Overview

## 童画奇旅 (WhimsyBrush)

A web application that transforms children's artwork into animated videos using AI technology.

### Core Value Proposition
Transform static children's drawings into dynamic, animated short videos with sound and music, creating a magical way to preserve and share childhood creativity.

### Target Users
- Primary: Parents of children aged 3-10 who enjoy sharing their children's achievements on social media
- Secondary: Early adopters interested in AI-generated content (AIGC) technology

### Key Features
- **Image Upload & Editing**: Support for JPG/PNG/HEIC formats with built-in cropping and rotation
- **AI Video Generation**: 5-second HD animations powered by Dreamina AI technology
- **Music Styles**: Five mood options (Joyful, Warm, Epic, Mysterious, Peaceful)
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices
- **User Authentication**: Email registration and Google login support
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure storage based on Firebase
- **Instant Sharing**: Video download and sharing capabilities

### Business Model
- MVP Phase: Free to validate market demand
- Future: Freemium model with premium features (higher quality, longer videos, advanced styles)

### Success Metrics
- User conversion rate (video generation / website visits)
- Share rate (share clicks / successful generations)
- Viral coefficient (K-Factor): new users from shares / sharing users
- Repeat creation rate: users creating multiple videos
